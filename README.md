### Hi there, I'm Li Zhaoyang

I am an **M.Sc. student in Computer Science and Technology** at the School of Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence, **Southwest Jiaotong University**. My research interests focus on **deep learning**, **multimodal learning**, **3D perception**, **point cloud understanding**, **robotics**, and **embodied AI**.

- I am currently working on **2D-3D representation bridging** and **point cloud completion**.
- My recent paper **SplAttN** was accepted to **ICML 2026** as a **Spotlight** paper.
- I am interested in algorithm engineering, quantitative research, and game technology.
- I am currently looking for internship opportunities.
- Reach me at [lzy11@my.swjtu.edu.cn](mailto:lzy11@my.swjtu.edu.cn) or [zhaoyang.li@centrale-med.fr](mailto:zhaoyang.li@centrale-med.fr).
